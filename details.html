Methodology
  1. Questionnaire design: Questions captured frequency of plastic use, types of plastic items, awareness of alternatives, willingness to change, and short feedback comments.
  2. Data collection: Google Forms filled by shoppers and vendors at local weekly markets (convenience sampling).
  3. Sample: 41 responses. (Add date range if you want to show collection dates.)
  4. Data processing: Export CSV → standardize response labels (e.g., \"Often\", \"Sometimes\", \"Rarely\") → remove internal score columns for public view.
Recommendations
  • Reusable-bag incentives: Offer discounts or loyalty points for shoppers who bring their own bags.
  • Market-level segregation: Place labelled bins for plastic and non-plastic waste near vendor rows and map collection points.
  • Awareness campaigns: Run demo stalls, signage, and short talks on market days that show reusable options and reuse/refill schemes.
  • Pilot exchange program: Collect clean plastics at a stall in exchange for small discounts at participating vendors.
How to cite this survey
